## Prerequisites

## Step 1: Setup TestNet Account

1. Install AlgoSigner extension
2. Create new account on TestNet
3. Get free TestNet ALGO from faucet:

## Step 2: Deploy Smart Contract

1. Connect wallet in Vaarush
2. Go to "Create Will" page
3. Fill in all details
4. Click "Deploy Contract"
5. Approve transaction in AlgoSigner
6. Contract deploys to TestNet!

## Step 3: Verify Deployment

Check your contract on Algorand Explorer:
